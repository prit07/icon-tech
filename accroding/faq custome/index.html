What Is a Gold IRA?

An Individual Retirement Account (IRA) is a savings program designed to help individuals plan for their retirement. Unlike conventional IRAs that allow investments in stocks, bonds, and certificates of deposit (CDs), a gold IRA allows investments in gold bullion, coins, and bars. These types of gold IRA investments can also be classified as contributions to an IRA during the fourth quarter.
