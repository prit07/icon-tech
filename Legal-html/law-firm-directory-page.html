The Law Office of Barry E. Janay, P.C. (“LOBEJ”) represents and counsels small to medium-sized businesses, individuals, and families in matters relating to estate planning, business law, wills, trusts, probate, real estate, and much more.

At LOBEJ, we will not rest easy unless we know that we have done the best possible job for our clients. Our loyalty is indivisible. Janay arrives at the office early and stays late. Our attorneys are skillful strategists who possess knowledge and abilities that will provide you with the assurance you need no matter what the situation that you find yourself in may be.

We Are Dedicated to Providing You With the Best Attorney for Your Case

The Law Office of Barry E. Janay, P.C. provides unique solutions tailored to meet its client’s needs. One size fits all is not the way that the Legal industry should be, unfortunately that’s the way too many lawyer’s and law firm’s treat their client’s.

At the Law Office of Barry E. Janay, P.C. we understand that you are an individual and you are unique, hence your legal needs will be just as unique. We won’t offer you one solution or way of handling your matter, we will offer a myriad of solutions.

We strive to provide you with top-notch legal representation for your case with INTEGRITY, COMMITMENT, INSIGHT AND RESULTS!
