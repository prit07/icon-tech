Why do you need it?

With a Justia Platinum Placement, you can achieve the maximum available visibility in the Justia Lawyer Directory and Legal Information Institute (LII) Lawyer Directory. Lawyers with a Justia Platinum Placement can rest assured that they will be seen first in their desired practice area and locale, as only one Justia Platinum Placement is available per practice area – metro combination.

Increased Visibility

Justia Platinum Placements allow you to maximize your visibility – more than 280x on average – with a fixed placement at the top of applicable Justia Lawyer Directory and LII Lawyer Directory listing pages. You will always appear above Justia Gold Placements and free listings in your chosen practice area – metro pair.

Enhanced Profile

With a Justia Platinum Placement, you receive an upgraded profile with a prominent contact form designed to make it easier to connect with potential clients. Competitor ads are also removed from this enhanced profile.

Traffic Stats

When you take advantage of Justia Platinum Placements, you also receive access to your traffic statistics on the Justia and LII lawyer directories and legal portals. In addition to the option to view your stats from the Justia Lawyer Directory dashboard, you will also receive a monthly report tracking profile impressions and page views across the Justia and LII properties.

Customized Landing Pages

Lawyers with Justia Platinum Placements on the Justia Lawyer Directory can take advantage of Justia Landing Pages, an optional benefit designed to increase client contacts. Utilizing Justia Landing Pages allows you to ensure that potential clients that see your Justia Premium Placement will be directed to a high-quality page designed for more leads and increased conversions.
